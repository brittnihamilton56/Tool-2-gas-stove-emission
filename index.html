Model + Assumptions

Single-zone indoor air model.

Well-mixed room Constant cooking emissions Ventilation + deposition removal
dC/dt = E/V − k(C − Cbg)
  • C = indoor PM2.5 concentration
  • E = cooking emission rate
  • V = room volume
  • k = total removal rate
  • Cbg = background PM2.5
  • ACH = air changes per hour, or how many times the air in the room is replaced in one hour
  • Breathing rate = assumed breathing volume used to estimate inhaled PM2.5 during and after cooking

The ventilation alternative increases particle removal. The induction alternative reduces emissions at the source.
